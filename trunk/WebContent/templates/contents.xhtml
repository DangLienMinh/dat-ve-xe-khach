  • October 2012

    Nemours Children's Hospital opens its state-of-the-art hospital at Lake Nona.

  • October 2011

    Lake Nona opens its newest neighborhood, Laureate Park.

  • August 2010

    UCF opens its Medical Education Building and Burnett Biomedical Research Building at Lake Nona.

  • May 2009

    Sanford-Burnham Medical Research Institute opens at Lake Nona.

  • January 2008

    Nemours announces Lake Nona as the location for its new children’s hospital in Orlando.

  • October 2007

    Construction commences on both the UCF Health Sciences Campus and the Burnham Institute’s campus at Lake Nona.

  • March 2006

    The Department of Veterans Affairs announces Lake Nona as the location for its Orlando VA Medical Center.

  • August 2006

    Burnham Institute for Medical Research announces its decision to locate an east coast research facility at Lake Nona. The Tavistock Group donates $...

  • October 2005

    UCF announces plans for a 50-acre Health Sciences Campus at Lake Nona which will include a College of Medicine.

  • April 2004

    Development begins at the VillageWalk and Waters Edge neighborhoods.

  • March 2004

    Lake Nona Golf & Country Club hosts inaugural Tavistock Cup.

  • March 2000

    NLP Community School receives the Urban Land Institute’s Award of Excellence for public/private partnerships.

  • August 1999

    Northlake Park Community School and YMCA is complete.

  • May 1998

    Development begins for Lake Nona’s second neighborhood, NorthLake Park.

  • September 1996

    Tavistock Group purchases the Lake Nona Development.

  • January 1986

    Lake Nona Golf & Country Club opens.
